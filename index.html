My Life

Hi! I’m Elena! If you would have told high school me that I was going to grow up to be a photographer, I would have been shocked. At the time I was studying law, thinking about joining the peace corps, and wanting to save the world by helping others. In a way that’s exactly what happened. I get to bring happiness and love to people everyday with my art, and isn’t that a way to save the world? I fell in love with photography the same way I fell in love with my husband, one day at a time and everyday all over again.
